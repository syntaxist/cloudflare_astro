# Getting Started with My Blog

Welcome to my blog! I've been meaning to start writing for a while now, and I'm excited to finally have a space to share my thoughts.

## Why Blog?

Writing helps me clarify my thinking. When I try to explain something in words, I often discover gaps in my understanding that I wouldn't have noticed otherwise. Plus, there's something satisfying about creating a resource that might help someone else.

## What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Technology** — tools, frameworks, and techniques I find interesting
- **Projects** — things I'm building and lessons learned along the way
- **Ideas** — thoughts on software development, design, and the web

## The Stack

This blog is built with [Astro](https://astro.build), styled with Tailwind CSS, and deployed on Cloudflare Pages. It's fast, simple, and exactly what I need.

